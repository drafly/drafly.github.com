Medical Artifical General Intelligence Lab

The Medical Artifical General IntelligenCe Lab (MAGICL) at BTBU is led by Longfei Han, Professor of Computer Science and, by courtesy, of Computer Electronic Information Engineering.

More

Our group's research develops Deep learning algorithms and Reinforcement learning algorithms to enable new capabilities in Medical image processing and healthcare. We have a primary focus on computer vision, such as image segmentation, image recognition, and image denoising, and we also study the latest content related to advertising recommendation predictions to understand and respond to human life behaviors from various types of data.
