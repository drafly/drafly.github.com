Medical Artifical General IntelligenCe Lab

The Medical Artifical General IntelligenCe Lab (MAGIC Lab) at BTBU is led by Longfei Han, Associate Professor at School of Computer Science, BTBU.

More

Our group's research develops Deep learning and Reinforcement learning algorithms to enable new capabilities in medical AI and healthcare. We have a primary focus on computer vision tasks, such as large-scale multimodal pretrainer, image recognition, and image segmentation, and developing AI algorithms to perform automated understanding and interpreting bioinformatics.

News & Updates
