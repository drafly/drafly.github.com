Hey! I am an associate professor at School of Computer Science, Beijing Technology and Business Unversity. I got my Ph.D. from the School of Information and Electronics, Beijing Institue of Technology, at fall 2012. I am working with Prof. Senlin Luo on machine learning and medical data mining. Since Sep. 2015, I am a visiting PHD student at Human Sensing Lab in Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). I am really fortunate to work with my advisor Dong Huang and Prof. Fernando de la Torre . After my graduation, I joint Tencent, and highly focus on Computational Advertising, especially on lift-time value prediction and dvertising recommendation system. My research interests include computer vision and large sacle recommendation system.
